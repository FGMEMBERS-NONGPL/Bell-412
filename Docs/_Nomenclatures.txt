* Meshes categories
1. Fuselage (main fuselage only outer parts, landing gears)
2. Doors (all doors inner and outer parts, hinges, handles)
3. Glasses (main glasses, frames)
4. Mechanic (rotors, blades, engines parts)
5. Cockpit (panel, pOH, pedestal, rudder, cyclic, collective, controls, instruments, seats)
6. Cabin (inner structure)
7. Lights (external lights)
8. Sensors (antenna, sensors, ...)
9. Misc (Handles, gutter, ...)

* Materials/Textures naming convention
- follow the meshes categories
